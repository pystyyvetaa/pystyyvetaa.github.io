Pystyy vetää

2019-02-04

We are a open CTF team from Winland, welcoming all who are interested in CTF to join us and learn together. Everyone from a beginner to a seasoned veteran is welcome.

Pystyy vetää aims to lower the barrier of entry for CTF events and to enable a more casual approach to participating. You don’t need to commit to allnighters or be a reverse engineering wizard to feel welcome.

We cherish our inclusive atmosphere, and harrassment isn’t tolerated. Don’t be a dick, and we <3 you.

If you would like to join us, send a Twitter DM our way, the DMs are open: https://twitter.com/pystyyvetaa
